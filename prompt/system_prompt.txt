Sattva – System Overview
Roles & Functionalities
1. Student

Registration & Profile

Register and login to the system.

Fill past information such as trauma, accidents, or mental health history.

Dashboard

View statistical insights about their mental health.

Features & Options

Calling: Call a helpline number where an AI responds.

AR Character: Interact with an AR-based emotional support character.

VR Environment: Enter immersive VR sessions (with mind sensors detecting emotions to customize the environment).

AI Video Consultation: Video call with an AI-powered virtual counselor.

Anonymous Calling: Connect with real psychiatrists anonymously (student only sees counselor’s ID, not personal details).

Volunteer Connection: Reach out to volunteers available in their college/university.

2. College / Institute

System Management

Handle entire system operations.

Add counselors (college clubs, societies, or groups).

Dashboard

View statistical reports of all students’ mental conditions.

Features

Add college advertisements.

Organize Yoga Sessions within the system.

VR Management: Configure VR sessions as per availability.

Manage online/offline counseling sessions and availability.

3. Volunteers / Counselors (in University)

Receive counseling requests from students.

Accept requests and reach out for counseling sessions.

4. Doctors / Psychiatrists

Receive counseling requests from students.

Accept and provide support to students.

Sessions remain anonymous (doctor’s personal details are hidden).

Additional Functionalities

Session Data Storage

All student sessions are stored in the database for tracking and statistical analysis.

Smart Volunteer Suggestion

Volunteers suggested to students based on location (hostel/room) and gender preferences.

Mind Sensor Integration

Before entering VR sessions, mind sensors analyze the student’s mental condition and emotions.

VR environments are customized accordingly.
